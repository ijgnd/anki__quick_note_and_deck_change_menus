This add-on has a very similar name and offers similar functionality to the popular add-on Quick note and deck buttons (Fork for 2.1). Check out the other add-on first. I made this add-on for me because "Quick note and deck buttons" doesn't work well for me on a small screen when I have multiple windows open so that the Add window is very narrow. # Configuration You can configure this add-on in the usual way from the add-on manager. There are multiple options you can adjust. You can assign the menu entries hotkeys by prepending "&" in front of a letter. # Bug Reports Use it at your own risk. Please do not use reviews for bug reports or support requests. I don't get notified of your reviews, and properly troubleshooting an issue through them is nearly impossible. Instead, please either use the issue tracker (preferred) or just message me on reddit (/u/ijgnord).
